Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations.

The following discussion summarizes the significant factors affecting our consolidated results of operations and financial condition for fiscal year 2026. Revenue grew 28% year over year, driven by sustained data-center demand and stronger-than-expected uptake of our enterprise platform.

We expect cautious growth in fiscal 2027 as channel inventory normalizes. Gross margin headwinds remain a near-term risk, particularly from rising HBM3e memory costs and Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) advanced-node wafer pricing. Microsoft and Alphabet remain our largest customers by revenue, together accounting for more than 30% of fiscal 2026 net sales.

Operating expenses grew faster than revenue due to elevated stock-based compensation and a one-time restructuring charge of $42 million recorded in the second quarter. We continue to invest in research and development, with capitalized software development costs of $118 million classified within property and equipment.

Forward-looking statements in this section reflect management's current views and are subject to material change.
